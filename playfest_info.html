Play Fest

School’s out and it’s time for fun with the family! With up to 200,000 anticipated participants, our annual line-up of Play events including Play Fest and Play Roadshows presents an exciting day out for the family during school holidays. Enjoy a fun-filled day with animation movie themed activities, games, contests supported by past partners including Air Selangor, Family Guard, Astro Shaw and Animonsta Studio. Families can even stand a chance to meet and greet your favourite movie characters!

Come enjoy our Play Fest events at selected mall concourse levels and Play Roadshows at selected GSC locations.
