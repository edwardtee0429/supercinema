GSC Play Events

School’s out and it’s time for fun with the family! With up to 200,000 anticipated participants, our annual line-up of Play events including Play Fest and Play Roadshows presents an exciting day out for the family during school holidays. Enjoy a fun-filled day with animation movie themed activities, games, contests supported by past partners including Air Selangor, Family Guard, Astro Shaw and Animonsta Studio. Families can even stand a chance to meet and greet your favourite movie characters! Come enjoy our Play Fest events at selected mall concourse levels and Play Roadshows at selected GSC locations.

GSC Popcorn Run

First launched in 2017, the GSC Popcorn Run has since been an annual event on our calendars! The fun-filled event is aimed at encouraging Malaysians to stay active whilst bonding over movies and popcorn with their loved ones and friends. Each year features a different movie, complete with a fiesta of food and drinks, movie-themed pit-stops, giveaways and a chance to meet your favourite movie characters! Each edition of the run features exclusive movie-themed medals and merchandise. 2022's edition of the run saw GSC's joint effort with Air Selangor to present the inaugural GSC X Air Selangor Hydro Run in collaboration with Mechamato's official movie partners Astro Shaw and Monsta, official venue partner IOI City Mall Putrajaya, and sponsors Applecrumby, McCain, Milo, Coca-Cola, V-Sure and Federal Flour Mills (FFM). With over 3,500 participants, families came together to bond over their favourite movie characters and popcorn, over a series of fun activities.
